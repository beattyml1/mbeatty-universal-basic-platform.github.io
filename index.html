Universal Basic Health Care

Publicly funded, privately administered. Universal coverage, individual choice

Health care will be funded through progressive federal taxes. Everyone will get a flat amount to put towards the plan of their choice. Those that don't elect a plan will be auto-enrolled in a plan chosen by their State. States will be expected to use the full allowance which should be enough cover a solid basic plan but there is nothing stopping them from adding additional funding a more premium plan from their own budget.

Health care Transparency

Health care provideres will be required to make their prices transparent and we will make sure they have the tools to do this well.

All the little things

From a tiered deductible, coinsuance plan, that encourages citizens to shop around without shopping around to fair market price regulations on perscription drugs, there are a million little details to what is wrong with health care right now and we'll have to dig into all of them if we want to fix it properly. You can see the start of that plan on the Healthcare Plan Details Page

Universal Basic Education

Yearly education allowance for all citizens

Every citizen of the United States will have a flat amount of money that they can access roughly equivalent to 4 years of an average State College or University. If they chose to go to a cheaper school they can save that money for later educational endevors

Additioanl allowance based on having both aptitude and need

Whether it's for Graduate level education or for a more exclusive and rigourous school we want to make sure that our best and brightest have the tools to build a better future for us regardless of their circumstances.

End predatory student loan programs

It was a good idea but between the fact that handing a highschooler an virtually unlimited amount of debt rarely turns out well and the fact that lobbyists have created a system of entrappment of vulnerable students we need to burn the system to the ground. All student loans will have the terms that keep the loans from be wiped out in bankruptcy removed.

Universal Basic Parental Support

Monthly child care allowance

All parents will have a flat per child, per month, child care allowance that should cover the curent going rate of basic child care. Today's kids will be the people that create the future we finish our lives in we owe it to ourselves to help make sure that families can thrive.

Guaranteed Parental Leave

All new parents will be guaranteed two weeks of paid time off and up to six months at a reduced rate. To avoid aggrevating the gender pay gap this will be applied to both genders.

Baby Boxes

All new mothers will receive a box full of essentials for there new infant. The box can double as a crib.

Subsidized Contraception

While all parents love is special, people make the best parents when they are ready to and want to have a kid. Let's make sure everyone has the tools ensure they become a parent on their terms.

Universal Basic Income (Eventually)

We will begin the large amount of planning and leg work that will eventually lead us towards a universal basic income. niversal basic income as a way to simultaneously raise standard of living for the working poor and middle class and to provide a safety net for those unemployed longer than short term unemployement insurance covers without encouraging people to stop working is about the only thing that conservative and liberal economists agree on.
